U.A. High is the #1 ranked high school for heroics and is considered as the top Hero Academy in Japan. Students are separated based on their abilities into specific Departments and Classes - A, B, C, D, E, F, G, H, I, J and K. At the entrance, there is a security wall nicknamed the "U.A. Barrier" that automatically closes if a person does not have a Student ID Card or Special Entry Permission ID. Many precautions have been made to keep members of the press and villains out. There are 5 competitions, we will only take these students which pass every five of them.

  1. First of all we look to your body and physical skills.
  2. Secondly we look to your brain skills. How are you in mathematics?
  3. Thirdly we test your rescuing methods and strategies.
  4. Fourthly we test your own skills to be a hero.
  5. At least if you pass all these competitions then you have to show us your fighting skills.

We show you why we want only the best hero's!

We are the best Highschool for Hero's. But of course you may have some doubts. But our motto is, nobody is perfect. But soon you will be!
